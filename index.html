About

Born and raised in the Inland Empire, Angelica Gutierrez has always been fascinated with creating and technology. Growing up during the rise of technology, Gutierrez found an interest in how the web and coding works.

Gutierrez studied at California State University, San Bernardino and received her Bachelor of Arts in Computer System, Graphics Programming with a minor in Web Graphic Design. During her time, she learned a lot about everything. From websites and mobile applications to video games and prototyping, she found her curiosity at a new height.

While in her undergrad, she was in her University’s Honors Program, where she took the challenge of creating a website for her program in a team. With no prior experience, she dedicated and self-taught herself the basics of HTML and CSS through various resources online. What started as a project for fun ended up as a project she led and worked on for 2 years. Starting as a team member and ending up as the team leader continued to spark her interest and ignited her to keep learning past graduation.

One year later, Gutierrez found herself continuing her learning and enrolling at Bellevue University through the Disney Aspire Program to receive her Bachelor of Science in Web Development. During her time, she learned a lot more about manipulating data and creating a database using Mongoose. Gutierrez was also able to learn how to use Angular applications and create components and APIs while using Jest and Jamine for testing.

Gutierrez hopes to continue her learning and keep her curiosity high in whatever her next adventure has to offer!
